TTSlow: Slow Down Text-to-Speech with Efficiency Robustness Evaluations

Xiaoxue Gao
Yiming Chen
Xianghu Yue
Yu Tsao
Nancy F. Chen
Haizhou Li

We show the audio samples, adversarial text samples and the number of frames for the synthesized speech samples of the following attack approaches.

Clean : Original clean audios from the datasets.

Text Baseline : Advasarial samples are generated from speaker baseline with speaker embedding input unchanged.

Speaker Baseline : Advasarial samples are generated from speaker baseline with text unchanged.

TTSlow-spk (l2) : Advasarial samples are generated from the proposed TTSlow-spk (l2).

TTSlow-spk (linf) : Advasarial samples are generated from the proposed TTSlow-spk (linf).

TTSlow-text : Advasarial samples are generated from the proposed TTSlow-text.
